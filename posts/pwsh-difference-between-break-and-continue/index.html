[PS Snippet] Difference Between Break and Continue

Jan 15, 2021 - powershell

The difference between break and continue.

  • break exit a loop or switch statement.
  • continue continue but skips the current iteration.
1switch (2,4,5)
2{
3  1 {"One"}
4  2 {"Two"; continue}
5  3 {"Three"}
6  4 {"Four"; break}
7  5 {"Five"}
8}

Output

1Two
2Four
